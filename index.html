Ways to Get Things Done
  • Dive In and Go
  • Plan Way in Advance
  • Work On It When You Find Time
  • Get Someone Else to Do It
  • Choose Not To Do It At All


Dive In And Go

Get started without a plan!

  • This method may be for those who like adventure.


Plan Way In Advance

Make sure you know every move you make before you start a project!

  • This could be a problem if you spend too much time planning and don't leave enough time for the project.


Work On It When You Find Time

Approrach it piecemeal!

  • This can be problematic for time management and could lead to greater errors due to lack of focus.


Get Someone Else To Do It

A time honored method for the lazy and dishonest!

  • One needs to weigh the risks of success and the consequences of getting caught.


Choose Not To Do It At All

Avoid what one doesn't want to do!

  • This may cause trouble depending on how important it is for the task to be completed.
